# 🇯🇵 JGB Yield Curve: 50 Years of BoJ Policy

Visualizing 50 years of Japanese Government Bond yield data 
from the Ministry of Finance.

## Charts
- **Historical Curve Comparison** — 7 key turning points overlaid
- **Yield by Maturity Over Time** — how each tenor evolved from 1990 to 2026

## Key Observations
- 35-year one-way decline from Bubble Era to YCC
- YCC (2016) artificially froze the short-to-mid curve
- 2022–2026: 35 years of suppression unwinding in 3 years

## Data Source
[Japan Ministry of Finance — JGB Interest Rate Data](https://www.mof.go.jp/jgbs/reference/interest_rate/)
